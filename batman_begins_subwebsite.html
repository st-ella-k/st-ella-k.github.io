Batman Begins

made in: 2005

This was one of Christopher Nolan's early movies.

It's the first movie of the Dark Knight trilogy, following the movies Dark Knight and Dark Knight Rises.

actors starring:

  • Michael Caine

  • Katie Holmes
  • Cillian Murphy

^ Christian Bale as Bruce Wayne

Wally Pfister was Nominated as Best Achievent in Cinematography in the Academy Awards (2006).

my personal opinion:

The movie was really good and the casting was immaculate. I really like the Dark Knight trilogy in general, so this was an easy 9/10.
