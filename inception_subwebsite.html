Inception

made in: 2010

Inception is a sci-fi movie, which was produced by C. Nolan's wife; Emma Thomas.

It has a very creative concept, like entering people's dreams. It was inspired by lucid dreaming, which Christopher Nolan experienced.

actors starring:

  • Leonardo DiCaprio
  • Elliot Page
  • Ken Watanabe
  • Joseph Gordon-Levitt
  • Tom Hardy
  • Marion Cotillard
  • Cillian Murphy
  • Dileep Rao

^ Joseph Gordon-Levitt and Leonardo DiCaprio as Arthur and Cobb

Inception won Best Achievement In Cinematography, Sound Editing, Sound Mixing and Visual Effects in the Academy Awards (2011).
