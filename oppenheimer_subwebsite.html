Oppenheimer

made in: 2023

Oppenheimer is a very recent work by Christopher Nolan. It was produced by Kai Bird and Martin Sherwin.

It's a biography about the scientist J. Robert Oppenheimer and his role in the development of the atomic bomb. It's set during WW2 and the bombing of Hiroshima.

my personal opinion:

I saw this movie in the cinema and it was the best film experience ever. The music was stunning, the scenes were literally breathtaking and I got occasional chills. Casting was perfect and this is easily the best movie I've seen all year.
